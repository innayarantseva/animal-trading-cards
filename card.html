Golden Retriever

Golden retrievers are considered to be the 4th smartest dog breed — in line behind Border Collies, Poodles, and German Shepherds.

  • Dog Breed Group: Sporting Dogs
  • Height: 1 foot, 9 inches to 2 feet tall at the shoulder
  • Weight: 55 to 75 pounds
  • Life Span: 10 to 12 years

Retrievers are the third most popular dog breed in America. They make great watch dogs, but not-so-great security dogs. They'll bark loudly at strangers, but once they physically interact with a person, a Golden becomes more friendly.
